Pizza Recipe

Description

This is an amazing recipe for homemade pizza that you will not regret making.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Semolina
  • Yeast
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Mix flour, semolina, water, and yeast to form dough
  2. Allow dough to rise for one hour
  3. After one hour, spread dough onto pizza pan
  4. Bake for 7 minutes at 450 degrees
  5. Spread baked dough with olive oil, garlic, and cover with mozzarella
  6. Bake for another 10 minutes at 450 degress
