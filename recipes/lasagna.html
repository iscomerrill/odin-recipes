Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is an amazing lasagna recipe that was passed down to me by my parents and before them from my grandparents. It is a delicious mix of tomatoes, meat, cheese, and pasta.

You should certainly make this recipe. Lasagna is the king of all Italian food and is extremely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of meat
  • 8 pieces of lasagna pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 28 oz of stewed tomatoes
  • various spices

Steps

  1. Chop up the onion
  2. Cook the meat and onion
  3. Boil water and cook the lasagna
  4. Layer the lasagna in a baking pan
  5. Place tomato and meat mixture between the lasagna pasta
