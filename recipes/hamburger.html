Hamburger Recipe

Description

This is an amazing recipe that creates a delicious burger. You should definitely try it out.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Chopped onions
  • Ketchup and Mayo

Steps

  1. Form the ground beef into patties
  2. Grill the patties until well done
  3. Place on buns and top with toppings
